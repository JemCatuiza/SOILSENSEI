Manual Fertilizer

Manual fertilizer application allows you to control the levels of essential nutrients for your plants. It involves the application of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium (N-P-K) in the right proportions to promote healthy plant growth.

Nutrient Information:

  • Nitrogen (N): Nitrogen is essential for leafy green growth. It helps plants produce chlorophyll, which is vital for photosynthesis.
  • Phosphorus (P): Phosphorus is crucial for root development, flowering, and fruiting in plants.
  • Potassium (K): Potassium is important for overall plant health, aiding in disease resistance and water uptake.
